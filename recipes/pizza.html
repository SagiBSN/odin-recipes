Bad pizza

Description

Disgusting pizza with kiwi, easy to bake and hard to eat!

You will hate this pizza and you can torture your kids with it by following this simple recipe.

  • flower (YES! We use flower and not flour)

  • Very bad mood!!

Steps

  1. cut the kiwis in halves (DO NOT peel them!)
  2. Put kiwis on pizza
  3. Throw it at your neighbor!
